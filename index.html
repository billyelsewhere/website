We're from Elsewhere

We’re a small, close-knit studio of design, strategy and tech people. We work with you to explore and understand the complex problems in your world, then we help you build the things that make your life simple again.

Embracing complexity

We live in a world filled with interesting challenges

Everyone has their own thing going on. Sometimes that brings it’s own flavour of problems that need solving. Knowing what to do, where to start, how to progress (not to mention where digital fits in) is tough.

The way we work launches us and you headlong into your complexities. We take the time to map out and explore all the ins and outs and make sense of it.

Getting going

We think big, but start (really) small.

Once we have a map of an entire situation, we make a plan around the smartest, most pragmatic first step forwards. It means starting out as reduced and clutter-free as possible. Literally just the bits we need to teach us something will do.

Iterating and tweaking

We welcome change and expect to adapt.

We design, build and ship things in small doses, testing in a controlled environment, and learning as we go.

As we move forward, we keep checking back to where we need to be and update the plan with what we learn accordingly.

Co creating and educating

We teach you the tricks of the trade

We think what we do works best when your entire company wants to change, so it makes sense to start with the people at the top.

Regardless of this, we’ll structure our work so that your team work alongside us and get involved as much as they can. That way over time you can learn to do things yourself or hire the right people in.

It’s more sustainable that way.

Doing our bit for good

We work for all the right reasons

Our focus is working on projects with a purpose of doing good in the world. We've structured our business on keeping our overheads low and our cost-modelling fair.

We will always strive to prioritise work which supports the UNs Sustainable Development Goals.

We would love to hear from you

Drop us a line so we can get together and understand your world in more detail..From there we can grab a coffee and have a chat, to see if there’s something we can help with.
