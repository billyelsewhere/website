Embracing complexity

If your problem was easy, you would have solved it yourself

To create something that makes sense and is really enjoyable to use, means fully appreciating the underlying complexity of the problem it solves.

Knowing where to start can be daunting for a lot of organisations, particularly when it comes to digital.

We always start by working with you to figure out and understand this complexity. We take the time to map it out and explore everything.

We put people first because they can often be the most complicated part.

Getting going

Always think big, just start (really) small.

Once you've helped us to understand your situation and we've mapped it out together, we make a plan around the smartest, most pragmatic first step forwards. It means starting out as clutter-free as possible.

The plan gives us a direction to go in, although the truth is we know this plan will change once we start and learn more.

Iterating and tweaking

Change is what makes it the best it can be.

We design, build and ship things in small doses, testing in a controlled environment, and learning as we go.

As we move forward, we keep checking back to where we need to be and update the plan with what we learn.

Co-creating and educating

You will learn with us

The work we do makes the most sense when an entire company adopts the same approach and mindset, so it makes sense to always work with decision makers close to the top.

Our way of working means your team is part of everything we do and is involved as much as they can be day to day. That way over time you can learn to do everything yourself or hire the right people in.

It’s more sustainable that way.

Doing our bit for good

We want to do work for the right reasons

Our focus is working on projects with a purpose of doing good in the world. We've structured our business on keeping our overheads low and our cost-modelling fair.

We will always strive to prioritise work which supports the UNs Sustainable Development Goals.
